Research

Cilia Gene Discovery

Our lab is primarily focused on unraveling the molecular mechanisms of rare diseases, with a specific emphasis on ciliopathies, a group of disorders associated with cilia dysfunction.

Learn more at CilioGenics.

Matching Variants

We are working on integrative bioinformatics approaches for variant analysis, including the development of tools like ConVarT.
